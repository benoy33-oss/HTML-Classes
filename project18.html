Hello, my name is Sam and this is my portfolio. I like making things out of random stuff and turning ideas into little projects. Sometimes it’s drawings, sometimes it’s things I build, and sometimes it’s just ideas I think are fun or interesting. Not everything works perfectly, but I still enjoy trying and learning as I go. I get distracted sometimes because I notice lots of small things, but that actually helps me come up with new ideas. I like being creative and trying different things, even if they’re a bit messy. This portfolio shows some of the things I have made and thought about.
