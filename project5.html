Facts About Cats

Cats spend about 70% of their lives sleeping, roughly 13-16 hours a day. Their brains are highly similar to humans, with identical regions responsible for emotions, showing advanced cognitive abilities. They are agile jumpers, capable of leaping up to six times their body length..

Cats have fewer taste buds than humans or dogs, around 473, and cannot taste sweetness. They can make over 90-100 different sounds, including meows, purrs, and chirps, and can even imitate human baby sounds to attract attention. Purring, produced by vibrating muscles in the larynx, may help cats relax, self-soothe, or even heal tissues.
